Summary

Money4you is a personal budgeting app. Your financial data — accounts, categories, transactions, budgets, reminders — is stored locally on your device in a private database. It is not sold, and it is not used for advertising. The only places your data leaves your device are:

  • Firebase Authentication, to sign you in.
  • Google Gemini, but only if you choose to connect your own free Gemini API key and use the free-form chat. Every other part of the AI assistant — ready-made questions, spending insights, tips — runs entirely on-device and sends nothing anywhere.

Data we collect

Account data

Sent to Firebase
What
Email address, and if you sign in with Google, your Google account's basic profile (name, email, profile photo URL).
Why
To create and authenticate your account, and keep your data separated from other users' data.
Where
Firebase Authentication (Google). Not shared with any other third party.

Financial data

On-device only

Accounts, income sources, categories, transactions, budgets, debts, tags, payment reminders.

What
Everything you enter into the app — account names and balances, category names/icons/budgets, transaction amounts/dates/notes, debt records, tags, and payment reminders you create.
Why
This is the core function of the app — tracking your finances.
Where
Stored only in a local Room (SQLite) database on your device. Money4you has no backend of its own to upload it to.
Backup
Settings → Data → "Export backup" writes this same data to a file you choose (e.g. via Android's file picker, which may save to local storage or a cloud app you control). We don't control where you save it or who you share it with afterward.

AI assistant

Two independent parts, very different data handling — only one of them ever sends anything off your device.

Ready-made questions

On-device

Tapping a suggested question ("How much did I spend on groceries?", spending by category, budgeting tips), plus everything else in the Assistant tab — trend pills, subscription detection, savings-goal progress — is computed entirely on-device. Nothing is sent anywhere. Works with no setup.

Free-form chat

Sent to Google, opt-in

Only works if you paste your own Gemini API key (aistudio.google.com), stored solely in the app's local settings — we never see it. Each message calls Gemini directly with your key, including a snapshot of your balances, budgets and recent transactions. This never passes through a server of ours; it's billed to your own key, and retention follows Google's Gemini API terms.

Notifications

On-device

Payment reminders you create are scheduled locally via Android's WorkManager and trigger a local notification on your device. No reminder data is sent anywhere.

App Check / Play Integrity

Device signal only

On release builds, the app uses Firebase App Check (Play Integrity) to prove sign-in requests come from a genuine, unmodified install. This exchanges device integrity signals with Google Play Services — it does not send your financial data, and it's unrelated to the AI assistant, which no longer goes through Firebase at all.

What we don't collect

  • No advertising identifiers, ad SDKs, or analytics/tracking SDKs
  • No location data
  • No contacts, camera, microphone, or SMS access
  • No sale of data to third parties, ever

Data deletion

Two ways to delete data in Money4you, depending on whether you want to keep your account.

Delete some of your data (keep your account)

In-app, instant
Steps
In Money4you, go to Profile → Settings → Danger zone → Delete all transactions, then confirm.
Deletes
All of your transaction history, permanently and immediately — this can't be undone.
Keeps
Your sign-in account, categories, accounts, and budgets. Balances reset to zero.
Retention
None — deletion is immediate, there's no backend copy to retain.

Delete your account and all data

Email request, 30 days
Steps
Email vitalijvivanov@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my account", sent from the address you signed up with.
Deletes
Your Firebase Authentication sign-in account and any data associated with it that we hold. Uninstalling the app additionally erases all financial data stored locally on your device immediately, since Money4you keeps no server-side copy of it.
Keeps
Nothing — this is a full deletion.
Retention
Processed within 30 days; we confirm by email once it's done.

Children's privacy

Money4you is not directed at children and is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will be revised. The app has no backend of its own, so we can't push in-app policy notifications — check this page for the latest version.
